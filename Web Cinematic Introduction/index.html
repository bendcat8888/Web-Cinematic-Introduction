Who We Are

InnoGen Pharmaceuticals, Inc. is a company that makes a difference in the pharmaceutical industry by improving existing products into new forms that are cost-effective and excellent in quality, benefiting the welfare of end-users.

InnoGen resulted from the fusion of nine Filipino-owned pharmaceutical companies, initiated in June 2010. Its areas of concentration include the Cardio-Metabolic Division, CNS and Gastro Division, and the Anti-infectives and Respiratory Division, supported by ethical marketing and national coverage.

Under The Big Tree

Across the company’s history, many stories, gatherings, and shared decisions have happened under the image of a big tree: a symbol of shade, continuity, and growth. It represents the people who gathered, the roots they protected, and the new branches they made possible.

Quality as a way of life

ISO quality certification signals to the global marketplace that a company follows recognized standards for dependable goods and services, verified by an independent third-party auditor. For a pharmaceutical manufacturer, that discipline carries special weight because quality is directly connected to human lives.

Lloyd Laboratories began its ISO 9002 preparation in October 1996 with management commitment, employee orientation, internal audit activity, regulatory affairs support, and coordinated quality leadership. After final audit activities in November 1998, Lloyd Laboratories marked a major quality milestone and later continued that commitment through ISO 9001:2015 certification.

The message is simple: quality is not treated as a one-time achievement. It is built into skilled people, documented systems, manufacturing standards, and the continuing responsibility to provide reliable pharmaceutical products and services.
