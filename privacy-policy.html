Privacy Policy

Data Privacy Notice & Consent

We protect the privacy and security of the personal information you share with us. This notice describes what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how you can contact us about privacy-related requests.

Summary

We collect only what is needed for registration, communication, and event or service management. We keep information for a limited time and do not use it for unrelated recurring marketing unless you explicitly opt in separately.

What we collect

Depending on the form, event, service, or inquiry, we may collect personal and professional details such as:

  • Full name
  • Contact details, including email address and phone number
  • Medical specialty or professional role, when relevant
  • Hospital, institution, company, or organization affiliation
  • Signature, attendance, or registration details, when required

Purpose of collection

We collect personal information for legitimate and necessary business purposes, including registration, event management, customer support, communication, compliance, and record keeping. Your data allows us to:

  • Maintain accurate records of attendees, customers, partners, or requestors.
  • Verify identity, professional credentials, or affiliation where applicable.
  • Log attendance or participation for professional credit, where applicable.
  • Respond to inquiries, provide requested materials, or send short-term follow-up communications.

Legitimate interests

We may use professional or inquiry-related information to improve InnoGen Pharmaceuticals Inc. events, services, customer support, and communication practices. Where possible, we use aggregated or anonymized information to understand audience needs and improve future activities.

Use of information & limitations

We will not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Use event or inquiry information for unrelated recurring marketing unless you explicitly opt in separately.
  • Contact you for unrelated purposes once the event, inquiry, or short-term follow-up is concluded.

Sharing & disclosure

We do not share your information outside InnoGen Pharmaceuticals Inc. except when needed for the purpose you provided it, when you have given consent, when trusted service providers support our operations under appropriate safeguards, or when required by law, regulation, or a lawful government request.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this notice, unless a longer period is required by law, regulation, audit, dispute resolution, or legitimate business need. Event records are typically retained for one year, after which they are securely destroyed, archived, or anonymized as appropriate.

Your rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have rights regarding your personal information. These may include the right to request access, request correction, object to certain processing, request erasure or blocking, and raise privacy-related concerns with us.

Consent

By submitting a form, registering for an event, sending an inquiry, or otherwise providing personal information to InnoGen Pharmaceuticals Inc., you acknowledge that you have read and understood this notice and consent to the collection and processing of your information for the purposes described above.
