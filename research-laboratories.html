The Lloyd Laboratories story reflects the discipline, quality systems, and regional manufacturing capability behind trusted pharmaceutical production.

Since our establishment in 1989

Lloyd Laboratories, Inc. has grown from modest beginnings in Marikina, Philippines into one of the country's leading contract toll manufacturers of pharmaceutical and related products.

From its Philippine base, Lloyd has become a major player in pharmaceutical manufacturing, with presence and related operations in the Philippines, Indonesia through PT Lloyd Pharma Indonesia, and India through Lloyd Laboratories Pvt. Ltd. / Sterile-Gene Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd.

Quality as a way of life

ISO quality certification signals to the global marketplace that a company follows recognized standards for dependable goods and services, verified by an independent third-party auditor. For a pharmaceutical manufacturer, that discipline carries special weight because quality is directly connected to human lives.

Lloyd Laboratories began its ISO 9002 preparation in October 1996 with management commitment, employee orientation, internal audit activity, regulatory affairs support, and coordinated quality leadership. After final audit activities in November 1998, Lloyd Laboratories marked a major quality milestone and later continued that commitment through ISO 9001:2015 certification.

The message is simple: quality is built into skilled people, documented systems, manufacturing standards, and the continuing responsibility to provide reliable pharmaceutical products and services.
